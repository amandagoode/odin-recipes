Zucchini Bread

Description

When I bake my grandmother's zucchini bread recipe it smells just like her house.

Ingredients

  • 3 slightly beaten eggs
  • 1 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla
  • 3 cups flour
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1 cup chopped nuts (I omit this part)
  • 2 full cups grated zucchini, including the peel
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon

Steps

  1. Combine eggs, oil and vanilla
  2. Sift dry ingredients together and add to first mixture
  3. Add zucchini and nuts (if using) and mix well
  4. Bake in greased loaf pan at 350 degrees for 1 hour or until toothpick inserted comes out clean
  5. Makes 2 loaves 5x9 inches or 4 loaves 7x4 inches
